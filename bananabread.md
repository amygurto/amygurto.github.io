# Banana bread

## Ingredients
* 265g self raising flour
* 40g flour
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 140g brown sugar
* 2 bananas
* 2 eggs
* 125ml cup skim milk
* 50g butter melted cooled

## Method
1. Preheat oven to 180c
2. Mash bananas
3. Combine flouts, cinnamon, sugar in a bowl
4. Add eggs, milk, butter. Stir. 
5. Add bananas

Bake 45-50 minutes
